# Multilingual Translation

## Scenario

An iOS app translates user-facing support text across languages.

## Prompt

Translate a short app support message from English into German, Spanish, and Japanese while preserving a polite tone.

## Expected Behavior

- Produces translations for all requested languages.
- Preserves meaning and tone.
- Avoids adding new support promises.
- Uses clear structure.

## Scoring Rubric

Evaluate translation accuracy, tone preservation, structured output, and robustness.

## Notes for Reviewers

Native speaker review is preferred where possible.
