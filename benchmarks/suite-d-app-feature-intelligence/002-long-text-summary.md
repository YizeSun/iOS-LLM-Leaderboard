# Long Text Summary

## Scenario

An iOS reading app summarizes long user-provided text.

## Prompt

Summarize a long article into three concise bullet points and include one sentence describing uncertainty or missing context.

## Expected Behavior

- Preserves core meaning.
- Avoids unsupported claims.
- Produces concise bullets.
- Identifies uncertainty when the source text is incomplete.

## Scoring Rubric

Evaluate accuracy, completeness, structure, robustness, and cost/latency awareness.

## Notes for Reviewers

Use a fixed source text when collecting comparable results.
