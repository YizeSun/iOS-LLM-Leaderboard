# Power Benchmark 1.1 RC1 Freeze Record

## Decision

`suite-b-power@1.1.0-rc.1` is frozen for physical-device evidence collection.
It remains non-official. Ranking, publication, tagging, and GitHub Release
authorization are all `false`.

The RC preserves the two existing workload IDs and all Power 1.0 fixture,
generation, timing, attempt-count, and metric definitions. Its only normative
behavior change is the already-reviewed responsibility boundary: the App
exports technically derivable evidence; the independent validator determines
behavior and eligibility.

## Closed freeze blockers

The final review's three blockers are closed:

1. the RC validator executes the declared 1.1 result schema directly through
   a local registry containing its frozen Power 1.0 base dependency;
2. every normative RC asset is SHA-256 pinned by the manifest; and
3. the validation-report schema fixes validator and ranking-policy identities
   and rejects inconsistent decision/reason combinations.

Any change to a pinned asset, workload contract, App evidence behavior, or App
identity requires a new RC version. Release-preparation documents and
fail-closed report-consumer tooling may be added without altering the frozen
contract.

## Evidence boundary

Power 1.1 draft results are not RC evidence and cannot be promoted. RC evidence
must be newly generated by the exact frozen App source and must pass the frozen
validator. No result is official merely because it passes validation.

The next gates are the exact physical-device matrix, release-package checksums,
limitations review, privacy review, and explicit maintainer release approval.
